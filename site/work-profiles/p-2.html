Brillo Construction Services

Brillo serves construction companies through out the Denver metro area. When you walk into a downtown skyrise the odds are you have seen their work.

They wanted a quick and simple website to accompany their active social presence. Bells and whistles were not something they were interested in. The key points were consice and to the point.

My goal was to keep everything on one page and not to direct anyone off the site in any way. Using modals as sections for information and galleries for images was a key role in keeping the visitors engaged. What Brillo was happy with was you can scan the website, get all the info you needed and onto contacting them with in a few min.

Project Details:

  • Bootstrap
  • Sass
  • One page layout
  • Modal service section
  • Easy sort image gallery
  • Photo retouching
